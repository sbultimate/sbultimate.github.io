Who Are We?

In addition to being the most fun club on campus, we are a collection of people from all athletic or non-athletic backgrounds who love playing ultimate frisbee! Many of us joined the club having never even played before, so absolutely everyone is welcome at practice! Who Can Join

Absolutely any stony brook student, undergraduate or graduate(or even faculty), is more than welcome here! We require absolutely no experience to join and will teach you everything you need to know!
If you do have experience playing ultimate, we do have a FILTHY A team with experienced coaches and players who would love to help bring your game to the next level.



About Panic

Panic is the name of our Men's A and B teams, as well as our co-ed team. All three teams compete at both local and away tournaments throughout the fall and the spring semesters. About Smash

Smash is the name of our BADASS Women's team. We are full of strong women who want to see the sport of ultimate grow for females! We go to local and away tournaments throughout both the fall and spring semesters. We also have our very own coach, Ken Cabot, as well as a wonderful lineup of 3 women captains, Ariana, Elana and Claire.



Practices

Practice times are located on our Schedule page. We hold practices 3 times a week in the evening during fall semester, usually located at the South P fields. Information on how to get there can be found on the How To Join page!. During the early spring semester due to frigid temperatures we hold practice indoors in the MAC courts, which are on the basement level of the Campus Recreation Center (big red building across from chemistry). These practice times are throughout the day depending on when we can reserve the space. During the later part of the spring semester we return to playing outdoors.



Tournaments

We usually go to about 4 tournaments per semester, mostly in the tri-state/New England area. During the fall we play at both Men's/Women's and Mixed (co-ed) tournaments, and during the Spring we primarily play Men's/Women's. During spring break we head down to Myrtle beach with two Mixed teams for the week to compete at High Tide!

How To Join

Practice

Probably the easiest way to join is to simply show up to practice! Information about when and where practice is held is located on our Schedule page. A campus map with bus routes is located to the right (or down below if you're on mobile) to assist you in getting there! For practices located at the South P fields, we recommend taking the express bus from the engineering loop.
When you come to practice, wear something that you're comfortable running around in, bring water for yourself, and bring cleats (highly recommended but not necessary).


Contact a Team Leader

If you have literally any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to reach out to one of the team leaders, all of whom are listed on the Contact page. We recommend using the team email: panicultimate@gmail.com for the quickest response as all team leaders have access, but a personalized message to anyone listed is also welcome!


Team Updates

Most of our team updates will be made via our Facebook Group, so we recommend joining that so that you don't miss out on any important announcements!

When to Join

Anyone can join at any time! We encourage new members to join early in the year, since practices are more geared towards teaching newer players then, however many of our current members have also joined at various times throughout the academic year.
