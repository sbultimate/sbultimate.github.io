Who We Are

We are Stony Brook University's official Ultimate Frisbee team. We have a men and women's team, and for several tournaments we combine to compete as a co-ed team. Both teams compete in USA Ultimate's Metro East Division 1 section for a ​national ranking. We mostly travel throughout the tri-state area for our weekend tournaments, and attend 8-10 events per school year.

Who Can Join

Any SBU student is welcome to join, both undergrad and graduate students! No prior experience is required! Our team is walk-on, and anyone who consistently shows up to practice will get at least one chance to go to a tournament or other Stony Brook Ultimate event!

All you have to do is visit our Contact page, and get in touch with one of our team leaders, or even just show up to practice! Practice times are located on the Schedule page.

About Stony Brook Ultimate


Who Are We?

In addition to being the most fun club on campus, we are a collection of people from all athletic or non-athletic backgrounds who love playing ultimate frisbee! Many of us joined the club having never even played before, so absolutely everyone is welcome at practice! We have both a men’s A team, B team and a women’s team, and occasionally we will combine the two and play co-ed. We attend a few tournaments each semester and hold practice 3 times a week, but you will definitely catch us tossing at on the staller steps daily(weather permitting).

Who Can Join

Absolutely any stony brook student, undergraduate or graduate(or even faculty) is more than welcome here! We require absolutely no experience to join and will teach you everything you need to know!

If you do have experience playing ultimate, we do have a FILTHY A team with experienced coaches and player who would love to help bring your game to the next level.

About Panic

Panic is the name of our mens A and B teams, as well as our co-ed team. All three teams compete at both local and away tournaments throughout the fall and the spring semesters. Our Men’s A team also competes at USA Ultimate sanctioned tournaments. Our Mens A team's coach is Matt LeMar of the AUDL’s New York Empire.

About Smash

Smash is the name of our BADASS womens team. We are full of strong women who want to see the sport of ultimate grow for females! We go to local and away tournaments throughout both the fall and spring semesters. We also have our very own coach, Ken Cabot, as well as a wonderful lineup of 3 women captains, Ariana, Elana and Claire.

Practices

Practice times are located on our schedule page. We hold practices 3 times a week in the evening during fall semester at south P fields, which are a quick walk or an even quicker bus ride on the express loop away. During the spring semester due to frigid temperatures we hold practice indoors in the MAC courts, which are on the basement level of the rect center(big red building across from chemistry). These practice times are throughout the day depending on when we can reserve the space, stay tuned!

Tournaments

We usually go to about 4 tournaments per semester, mostly in the tri-state/New England area. During the fall we play at both Men's/Women's and mixed tournaments, and during the Spring we primarily play Men's/Women's. During spring break we head down to Myrtle beach with two mixed teams for the week to compete at High Tide!
